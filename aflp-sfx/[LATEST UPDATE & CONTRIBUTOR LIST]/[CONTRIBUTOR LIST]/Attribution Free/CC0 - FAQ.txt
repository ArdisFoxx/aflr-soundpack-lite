What is CC0?
Creative Commmons 0. Public domain. Audio released under creative commons liscense that does not need to be attributed.

Why CC0?
Other liscenses involve attribution and we deal in NSFW...
Which is a ethical concern in that many creators may not want to know sounds are used for NSFW. 
We chose to branch out to CC0 material to expand libraries [cost & time constraints] but also to protect all works published using the sound pack.
Ultimately we will only use liscenses that are "public domain" in order to avoid concerns.
